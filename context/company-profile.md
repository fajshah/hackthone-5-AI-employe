# TechNova Company Profile

## Overview
TechNova is a fast-growing SaaS company founded in 2021, specializing in AI-powered project management solutions. Headquartered in Austin, Texas, with remote teams across North America, Europe, and Asia, TechNova serves over 15,000 businesses worldwide. Our mission is to simplify project workflows and boost team productivity through intelligent automation.

## Team Structure
- **Total Employees:** 180+
- **Engineering Team:** 65 developers and QA engineers
- **Customer Success:** 45 support agents and success managers
- **Sales & Marketing:** 40 professionals
- **Product & Design:** 20 team members
- **Operations:** 10 staff

## Target Customers
TechNova primarily serves:
1. **Small Businesses (10-50 employees):** Startups and small agencies needing affordable project tracking
2. **Mid-Market Companies (50-500 employees):** Growing teams requiring advanced collaboration features
3. **Enterprise Organizations (500+ employees):** Large corporations needing custom integrations and dedicated support

## Current Pain Point
TechNova is experiencing **customer support overload**. With rapid user growth (300% YoY), the support team is overwhelmed with 2,000+ weekly tickets across email, WhatsApp, and web forms. Common issues include:
- Password resets and account access (35%)
- Feature how-to questions (25%)
- Billing and subscription queries (20%)
- Bug reports and technical issues (15%)
- Integration setup help (5%)

Average response time has increased from 4 hours to 18 hours, causing customer satisfaction scores to drop from 4.8 to 4.2. The company needs an AI-powered triage system to categorize, prioritize, and draft responses for common queries while escalating complex issues to human agents.
