# TechNova Brand Voice Guidelines

## Overview
TechNova's brand voice is **friendly-professional, empathetic, and efficient**. We adapt our tone based on channel and customer situation while maintaining consistency in our core values.

---

## Core Brand Principles

### 1. Friendly-Professional
We are approachable but competent. Warm but knowledgeable.

**Do:**
- "Hi there! I'd be happy to help you with that."
- "Great question! Let me look into this for you."
- "Thanks for reaching out. I understand how important this is."

**Don't:**
- "Per our policy..." (too formal/cold)
- "Hey dude, what's up?" (too casual)
- "This is a known issue." (dismissive)

---

### 2. Empathetic
We acknowledge customer emotions and validate their concerns.

**Do:**
- "I completely understand how frustrating this must be."
- "That doesn't sound like the experience we want for you."
- "I can see why this would be concerning, especially with your deadline."

**Don't:**
- "Calm down." (dismissive)
- "This is not our fault." (defensive)
- "Other customers don't have this problem." (invalidating)

---

### 3. Concise & Clear
We respect customer time. Get to the point without being rude.

**Do:**
- "Here's what I found: [answer]. Next steps: [action]."
- "Short answer: Yes, you can do that. Here's how: [steps]."

**Don't:**
- Write 500 words when 50 will do
- Use jargon without explanation
- Bury the lead in unnecessary context

## Channel-Specific Guidelines

### Email (Long-form, Detailed)

**Tone:** Professional, thorough, structured
**Length:** 150-300 words typical
**Format:** Greeting → Acknowledgment → Answer → Next Steps → Closing

**Example:**
```
Hi Jennifer,

Thank you for reaching out about the SSO integration issue. I understand this is blocking your company-wide rollout, and I want to get this resolved quickly.

I've reviewed your SAML configuration and found the issue. The metadata XML error occurs when the Entity ID format doesn't match our expected structure. 

Here's what to check:
1. Ensure your Entity ID uses HTTPS (not HTTP)
2. Verify the NameID format is set to "emailAddress"
3. Confirm the ACS URL matches exactly: https://app.technova.com/auth/saml

I'm escalating this to our Enterprise Support team. They'll schedule a call with your Okta admin within the next 4 hours to complete the setup.

Ticket reference: #ENT-2026-0315

Best regards,
TechNova Support Team
```

---

### WhatsApp (Short, Casual, Quick)

**Tone:** Conversational, friendly, efficient
**Length:** 1-3 sentences, under 100 characters ideal
**Format:** Direct answer + emoji (sparingly) + offer for more help

**Example:**
```
Hey Alex! 👋 To add someone to your project:
1. Open the project
2. Tap "Members" 
3. Enter their email
4. Choose their role

That's it! Need help with anything else?
```

**WhatsApp Do's:**
- Use contractions: "you're", "we'll", "don't"
- Emojis OK but limited: 👋 ✅ 🎉 🙏
- Casual greetings: "Hey", "Hi there"
- Quick acknowledgments: "Got it!", "On it!"

**WhatsApp Don'ts:**
- Long paragraphs
- Formal language: "Dear Customer", "We regret to inform"
- Multiple emojis per message
- Technical jargon without explanation

---

### Web Form (Structured, Informative)

**Tone:** Professional but approachable
**Length:** 100-200 words
**Format:** Acknowledge → Answer → Resources → Contact

**Example:**
```
Hi Nathan,

Thanks for reporting the dashboard widget issue. This sounds frustrating.

Our team is investigating this bug. In the meantime, please try:
1. Hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try incognito/private mode

If the issue persists, reply with:
- Browser version
- Screenshot of the error
- Console logs (F12 → Console tab)

We'll prioritize this for you.

Best,
TechNova Support
```

## Situation-Specific Tone Adjustments

### Bug Reports (Technical Issues)

**Approach:** Acknowledge impact → Investigate → Update → Resolve

**Example:**
```
Hi Daniel,

I'm sorry you're experiencing upload issues on iOS. This is definitely not the experience we want for your art team.

I've reproduced this on our end and filed it as a priority bug (MOB-4821). Our mobile team is investigating.

Temporary workaround: Use the web app at app.technova.com on mobile browser - uploads work there.

We'll update you within 24 hours with a fix timeline.

Thanks for your patience!
```

---

### Billing Issues (Sensitive)

**Approach:** Empathetic → Clear → Action-oriented

**Example:**
```
Hi Robert,

I completely understand your concern about the duplicate charge. That's definitely not right, and I apologize for the confusion.

I've located both transactions (TXN-8847291 and TXN-8847292). I'm escalating this to our billing team to:
1. Investigate how this happened
2. Process the refund for the duplicate charge
3. Ensure it doesn't recur

You'll receive confirmation within 2 business hours, and the refund will appear in 3-5 business days.

Again, I apologize for this error.

Best regards,
TechNova Billing Support
```

---

### Angry Customers (De-escalation)

**Approach:** Validate → Apologize → Act → Assure

**Example:**
```
Hi Michael,

You're absolutely right to be frustrated. Performance issues with large projects are not acceptable, especially when it affects your daily operations.

I sincerely apologize for this experience. This is not the quality we strive for.

I'm escalating this to our Engineering Lead as a priority issue. They will:
1. Investigate your specific projects
2. Optimize your account configuration
3. Provide a timeline for improvements

You'll hear from our Senior Support Lead within 2 hours.

We value your business and will make this right.

Sincerely,
TechNova Support Team
```

---

### Feature Requests (Positive but Realistic)

**Approach:** Appreciate → Acknowledge → Set expectations

**Example:**
```
Hi Amanda,

That's a fantastic suggestion! Custom validation rules would definitely help teams maintain data consistency.

I've logged this in our product feedback board (FR-2026-0412). Our product team reviews these requests weekly.

While I can't commit to a timeline, I can tell you that form customization is an area we're exploring for Q3 2026.

Workaround for now: Use required fields and naming conventions in your custom field descriptions.

Thanks for helping us improve TechNova!
```

---

### Cancellation Requests (Retention-focused)

**Approach:** Understand → Offer alternatives → Respect decision

**Example:**
```
Hi Rachel,

I'm sorry to hear you need to cancel. I understand budget cuts are challenging, and I appreciate you being a loyal customer for 3 years.

Before we proceed, I want to mention:
- We offer a "Data Archive" plan at $29/month for read-only access
- You can export all your data anytime before cancellation
- If you resubscribe within 6 months, we can restore your data

If you'd like to proceed with cancellation, I can help with that too. Just let me know.

Either way, thank you for being part of TechNova.

Best regards,
TechNova Customer Success
```

## Language Preferences

### Use These Words:
- "I'd be happy to..."
- "Let me..."
- "I understand..."
- "Great question..."
- "Thanks for..."
- "I can help with..."
- "Here's what I found..."
- "Next steps..."

### Avoid These Words:
- "Unfortunately..." (use "Here's the situation...")
- "Policy" (use "guidelines" or "how this works")
- "Can't" / "Cannot" (use "Here's what we can do instead...")
- "You need to..." (use "Here's how to...")
- "Actually..." (sounds condescending)
- "Just..." (minimizes customer concern)

---

## Personalization Guidelines

### Use Customer Name
- First mention: "Hi [First Name]"
- Subsequent: Can omit name or use once more

### Reference Their Context
- "I see you're on the Pro plan..."
- "Since you're managing 50+ projects..."
- "For your use case with [specific need]..."

### Match Customer Tone (Mirroring)
- Formal customer → Slightly more formal
- Casual customer → Friendly and relaxed
- Urgent customer → Quick and action-oriented

## Response Time Expectations in Voice

Always set clear expectations:

**Email:** "You'll hear back within 24 hours"
**WhatsApp:** "I'll check and get back to you in a few minutes"
**Web Form:** "We typically respond within 4 hours"

If delayed:
```
Hi [Name], just wanted to update you that we're still working on this. 
Our team needs a bit more time to investigate. I'll have an answer by [specific time]. 
Thanks for your patience! 🙏
```
